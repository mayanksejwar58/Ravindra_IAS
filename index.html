Frequently Asked Questions

❌ No matching questions found.

What is the duration of the GS Foundation PCM course? +

The GS Foundation Prelims-cum-Mains course spans 10 months covering the complete UPSC CSE syllabus — NCERTs, all 4 GS Papers, CSAT, Essay, and Ethics — with an integrated Prelims and Mains Test Series throughout.

Are online classes interactive or just recorded? +

All LIVE online sessions are fully interactive with real-time Q&A via chat or audio, polls, and dedicated doubt-clearing sessions. Classes are also recorded and available for 6-month replay access on our student portal and app.

What study materials are included in the course? +

Students receive: (1) Printed GS notes covering 450+ topics, (2) Monthly Current Affairs magazine, (3) PYQ compilation with model answers, (4) Mind maps for GS I–IV, and (5) Full digital access to 2000+ video lectures, daily quiz bank, and our online test portal.

Are scholarships available? How do I apply? +

Yes. We offer merit scholarships of 25%, 50%, or 75% based on our entrance scholarship test. Special concessions for SC/ST/OBC candidates, women aspirants, defence families, and repeat attempt candidates. Contact us through the enrollment form for scholarship test details.

What optional subjects are offered? +

We currently offer specialized 4.5-month optional programmes in: Sociology, Political Science & IR (PSIR), Geography, and Anthropology. Each includes teaching sessions, 5 full-length tests, individual answer evaluation, and PYQ discussion sessions.

How is mentorship structured at Ravindra IAS? +

Every enrolled student is assigned a dedicated mentor within the first week — typically a senior faculty member or cleared civil servant. Fortnightly 45-minute 1-on-1 sessions covering progress review, study plan refinement, answer writing evaluation, and motivation throughout preparation.
